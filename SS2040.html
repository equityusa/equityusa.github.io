It's time America has a party for the people.

The Equity Party was founded in 2023 by Sydney Satalino, a college student dissatisfied with the corrupt state of politics in the United States of America. Eager to topple the two-party system and create real change in America, what began as just a club of Sydney's closest internet friends has since evolved to be a community hellbent on changing America for the better - one with big aspirations for the changes they can create and the impact they can have on American society as a whole.
